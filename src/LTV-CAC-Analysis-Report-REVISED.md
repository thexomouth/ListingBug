## 🎯 Critical Insight: Understanding Seller vs. Buyer Side Services

**When a property is JUST LISTED = SELLER SIDE**
- Seller has already decided to list
- Listing agent needs services NOW to make property show-ready
- Time-sensitive window: 24-72 hours for maximum impact

**When a property goes UNDER CONTRACT = BUYER SIDE**
- Buyer needs inspections, insurance, moving services
- These services happen LATER in the transaction (not immediate)

**This changes everything about who gets the most value from ListingBug.**
